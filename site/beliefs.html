Indoguna

Indoguna Singapore is all about good food, good wine and good company.

For over 20 years, we’ve been at the forefront of the food and beverage industry, dedicated to sourcing the finest the world has to offer and serving it to you. Pursuing only the best – whether it’s meats, seafoods or boutique wines, we have everything you could wish for. But that’s not all we can do. As a fully operational butchery, we offer value-added services such as meat and seafood value adding and portion control. It doesn’t stop there though. Understanding that passion for food means you only want the finest. Greengrocer.com.sg was started in 2007, to reach out to the home consumers who have a taste for quality produce. Our philosophy as always – only the best is good enough.

Indoguna Singapore is also about standards, certifications and quality assurance.

Our commitment to offer only the best quality produce to our customers is regularly evaluated & certified by independent bodies and government agencies (AVA Licence). Indoguna has also been assessed by Bureau Veritas Certification and conforms to the requirements of the Marine Stewardship Council standard for Chain of Custody, in the distribution of sustainable seafood. Because we believe that a safe and healthy workplace is primordial in our business, we’ve enrolled in a 5-step programme with WHS council and thus been granted BizSAFE level 3. In addition to the above, Indoguna is certified ISO 22301:2012 for Business Continuity Management System (BCM Certificate)

But that is not all that we are concerned about.

Here at Indoguna, we care for the environment. Indoguna is committed to operate its business in an environmentally responsible manner. We recognize that reducing the environmental impact of our products and operations is an important part of the value we deliver to our customers. We strive to train and work with our employees, our business partners and our community to achieve environmental excellence and to foster an understanding of the importance of making environmentally responsible decisions. Our Belief

Indoguna

There are three things that we firmly believe in, and this is what makes Indoguna the best choice.

Quality

Sourcing from around the globe for the best the world has to offer is a passion that comes with our business. We are dedicated to provide only the best the world has to offer. Our never ending pursuit to quality produce is our dedication to our customers that we continuously provide surprise ingredients, hard to find produce and produce from well renowned farmers, fishermen, artisan producers. Our quality dedication doesn’t stop there. In 2008, we were certified with ISO 9001:2008, giving our business a structured form of guidance on quality management. In 2014, we were certified with ISO 22000:2005, which specifies requirements for a food safety management system where an organization in the food chain needs to demonstrate its ability to control food safety hazards in order to ensure that food is safe at the time of human consumption. Our certification conforms with both statutory and regulatory requirements, and with mutually agreed food safety requirements of customers. This is communicated, implemented and maintained at all levels of Indoguna. Because really, if it’s something Indoguna believes in, then you know you can too. Click Here to learn more about our Quality & Food Safety Policy.

Food Safety

Food safety is our priority. Together with our philosophy of ONLY THE BEST IS GOOD ENOUGH, it is our business culture that these two elements go hand in hand. We are certified ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 22000:2005 throughout our warehouse, delivery and production areas; giving you the peace of mind that our produce are not only good to be consumed but they are SAFE to be consumed. Our certification conforms with both statutory and regulatory requirements, and with mutually agreed food safety requirements of customers. This is communicated, implemented and maintained at all levels of Indoguna. Our year on year “A” grading from AVA is another standard reassurance that you have nothing to worry about when it comes to food safety. In 2015, we were awarded the AVA Food Safety Excellence Scheme, Bronze Award, for achieving "A" grading for 5 consecutive years. We are committed to only the best.

The Customer Is Key

Our commitment to our level best in satisfying all needs and wants is our specialty. We will endeavor all channels to provide our customers what they are looking for. As they say, ‘the world is your oyster’….
