Microsoft Challenge

Strategy

The Worldwide Creative Day (WCD) is a global event happening on one day. The aim is to draw the biggest digital fresco never created by students from the entiere world. Students from selected universities in different contries draw on a same digital platform through the SurfaceBook Pro. The creation can be watched on any terminal but it's possible to draw only during the pop ups in universities trough the SurfaceBook. Each drawings can be highlighted on mouseover and target the student portfolio in the aim to be detected by employers.

During the final presentation to Microsoft, my crew was standing in Paris, while I was presenting from the Microsoft world headquarters in Seattle.
